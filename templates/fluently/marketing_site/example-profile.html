About Me

Hi there, I'm Susan! I'm an SLP from Boston that loves helping children overcome their speech disorders and become confident speakers. I've been working in stuttering therapy for the past 22 years.

Certifications

-Board-certified specialist, American Speech & Hearing Association
-Member, Telepractice Special Interest Group (ASHA)
-Member, National Stuttering Association

Experience

-20+ years specializing in fluency disorders
-MS Emerson College
-Ph.D Boston University

Therapy Approach

I love to understand a client's goals before beginning therapy--it's important to develop a process so we can achieve the best results together. I base my therapies on existing research, like van Riper, as well as the Lidcombe approach to treating stuttering.
