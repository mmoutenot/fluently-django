Our Mission

Let's transform your speech therapy experience

Fluently is designed to help you find the right speech therapy options for your needs. We believe that speech therapy can change lives. We know this impact from firsthand experience—from stuttering therapy to autism-spectrum disorder therapies. The Fluently team launched out of Tufts University, with three undergrad students interested in combining the worlds of computer science, child development and personal improvement.
Now we're focused on improving the speech therapy experience for everyone involved: students, parents and speech-language pathologists. Oftentimes, speech therapy is the single most effective aspect of treating speech, language and social disorders. Shouldn't it be easier to find and receive the right type of therapy for your needs?

We think so. That's why we're building Fluently.
